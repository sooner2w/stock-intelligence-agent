# Loom Script — Stock Intelligence Agent
## 60 seconds. No edits. One take.

---

## BEFORE YOU HIT RECORD

**Setup checklist:**
- [ ] Dashboard running at localhost:8501 — already on the Research tab
- [ ] Ticker input is blank (clear it before recording)
- [ ] Browser is full screen, no other tabs visible
- [ ] Discover mode sidebar visible on the left
- [ ] System notifications turned off (Do Not Disturb on)
- [ ] Options section pre-scrolled out of view (so the scroll feels natural)

**Tone:** Confident. Calm. Let the product do the work.
Not a tutorial. Not a walkthrough. A demonstration.
Speak like someone showing a colleague something they built — not presenting to a panel.

---

## THE SCRIPT

---

**[0:00–0:06] — THE HOOK**
*(Screen: blank dashboard, ticker input empty)*

> "This is a stock research tool I built from scratch.
> It reads SEC filings, scores options flow, and tracks insider trades —
> all from free public data."

*Don't rush. Let those three data sources land.*

---

**[0:06–0:10] — TYPE THE TICKER**
*(Type "NVDA" into the input and hit Analyze. Watch it load.)*

> "I'll show you what it does in sixty seconds."

*Nothing else. Let the load happen. It feels deliberate.*

---

**[0:10–0:22] — THE SIGNAL BAR**
*(Scroll just enough to show the 5 signal cards)*

> "First thing you see is a signal summary —
> valuation, growth, analyst consensus, momentum, short interest.
> Color-coded. No interpretation needed."

*(Pause one beat on the cards.)*

> "Below that — insider trades pulled directly from SEC EDGAR.
> Every executive buy and sell, within 48 hours of when it happened."

*(Scroll slowly to show the insider trades list and the buy/sell ratio)*

---

**[0:22–0:38] — OPTIONS FLOW + CONVICTION SCORE**
*(Scroll to the Options Flow section. The metrics bar should be visible.)*

> "This is where it gets interesting.
> Put/call ratio. Max pain. IV smile. Open interest by strike."

*(Pause on the ⚡ unusual activity expander — click to open it if it's not already)*

> "When a contract shows volume three times its open interest —
> fresh money coming in — it gets flagged here."

*(Point at the conviction score badge)*

> "And instead of just showing you the signal,
> it scores it against six other independent signals —
> insider activity, fundamentals, momentum, analyst consensus —
> and gives you a conviction score out of ten."

*(Read the score and the action text below it)*

> "Eight out of ten. Signals aligned. Worth researching."

---

**[0:38–0:52] — DISCOVER MODE**
*(Click "Discover Picks" in the sidebar. Let the scanner run — the progress bar is good, leave it.)*

> "And if you don't have a ticker in mind —"

*(Wait for the top 3 cards to appear)*

> "— it scans eighty stocks across seven sectors automatically.
> Scores each one. Surfaces the top picks."

*(Gesture at the cards — don't name them, let the viewer see)*

> "No ticker required. Just open it."

---

**[0:52–1:00] — THE CLOSE**
*(Stop scrolling. Hold the screen still on the Discover results.)*

> "The whole stack — agent, memory, SEC parser, options scoring —
> built from scratch in Python.
> No frameworks. No paid data sources."

*(One beat of silence.)*

> "This is what the designer-builder combination looks like."

*(Stop recording.)*

---

## DIRECTOR'S NOTES

**The silence at the end is intentional.** Don't fill it. Don't say "so yeah" or "anyway." Stop talking the moment you say "designer-builder combination" and hit stop. The pause before you end is confidence.

**Don't explain what you're clicking.** "Now I'm going to click here and you can see that..." — cut all of that. Just click it. The viewer can see your screen.

**The conviction score is the money shot.** Slow down there. Point at the number. Read the action line ("Signals aligned. Worth researching."). That's the thing no other free tool does and it should feel that way.

**If the scanner takes a while:** That's fine. Don't apologize for it or fill the silence with "it's loading because..." Just say "it scans eighty stocks across seven sectors" while it runs. The wait actually sells the depth of what it's doing.

**One take is better than edited.** A polished Loom with cuts feels like a commercial. One clean take feels like a founder. You want to feel like a founder.
